About Qiayun Electric Power

Shanghai-based global intelligent power solution provider and full-service partner

Shanghai Qiayun Electric Power Service Co., Ltd. was established in 2018 and integrates R&D, production, sales, and full-process technical services.

The company focuses on high & low voltage complete equipment manufacturing and smart power system solutions, supported by a self-built 4,000㎡ production base and 82+ professional technicians.

We have served 1,000+ clients and built a Shanghai 30-minute rapid response service system to support multinational factories, industrial parks, and commercial institutions.

History & Key Moments

From Shanghai to the World

With 11 years of deep focus in the power industry, we have grown from hands-on operators to a full-chain power service provider.

2015

The Start

Served over a thousand industrial and commercial clients in Shanghai, including multinationals, state-owned enterprises, and listed companies. Achieved 100% client satisfaction and earned strong recognition from local power authorities and our clients.

2016–2017

Standards & Innovation

Benchmarked against international standards. Established protocols for electrical testing and managed services. Pioneered smart power IoT, developed 10KV emergency repair services, and built our own spare parts warehouse — significantly improving response times.

2018–2019

Digitalization & Full-Chain

Introduced a smart power platform for intelligent, refined operations management. Integrated resources from design institutes and equipment manufacturers, building a collaborative industry chain to offer end-to-end power solutions.

2020–2022

Digital Outreach & Certification

Designed a dedicated power App to reach high-end clients precisely. Achieved full-team certification and obtained industry licenses from the Construction Commission and East China Energy Bureau — ensuring our services meet international standards.

2023–2024

Building Manufacturing Capabilities

Set up our own equipment factory, secured full production certifications, and began operations. Entered the power design institute space, achieving full-chain control from design and manufacturing to operations and maintenance.

2025

Online-Offline Integration

Launched our power App with a dedicated operations team. Integrated online and offline services to precisely meet the needs of multinational clients.

We stand by our “zero mistakes” commitment. Our services span five core areas: design, equipment, operations, IoT, and digital platforms. In 2026, we will connect global power resources to serve high-end international clients.
